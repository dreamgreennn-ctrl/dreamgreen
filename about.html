Our Story

Born in Surat, rooted in love — Dream Green is on a mission to make plant gifting joyful, beautiful, and effortless.

How Dream Green Began

It started with a single potted plant wrapped in brown paper and a handwritten note. In 2023, we gifted a friend a Snake Plant for her new apartment in Adajan, and her reaction was priceless. "No one's ever given me a plant before," she said, tearing up.

That moment sparked an idea: what if we could make plant gifting as beautiful and exciting as flower gifting — but with a gift that lasts years, not days?

Dream Green was born right here in Surat, Gujarat. We started by hand-delivering plants on our scooter. Today, we've sent over 5,000 plant gifts across the city — for birthdays, housewarmings, anniversaries, festivals, and those beautiful "just because" moments.

Delivery & Shipping

📍 Delivery Area

We currently deliver across Surat, Gujarat only. This includes all major areas: Adajan, Athwa, City Light, Vesu, Pal, Piplod, Ghod Dod Road, Varachha, Katargam, Rander, Udhna, Sarthana, Magdalla, Dumas Road, and more.

🚚 Same-Day Delivery

Order before 2:00 PM for same-day delivery. You can also schedule delivery for a future date and select a preferred time slot (morning, afternoon, or evening).

💰 Delivery Charges

Free delivery on all orders above ₹499. For orders below ₹499, a flat ₹49 delivery charge applies. All plants are carefully packed in shock-resistant boxes to ensure they arrive safely at the doorstep.

🔄 Returns & Replacements

We offer a 100% live plant guarantee. If your plant arrives damaged or doesn't survive within the first 7 days, we'll replace it for free. Just share a photo with us on WhatsApp.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you deliver outside Surat?
Currently, we deliver only within Surat, Gujarat. We're working on expanding to other cities in Gujarat soon. Follow us on Instagram for updates!
Can I send a plant gift without the recipient knowing who it's from?
Absolutely! We can keep your identity anonymous and only include the gift message. Just mention this in the gift message or let us know on WhatsApp.
What if the recipient isn't home?
Our delivery partner will call the recipient before arriving. If they're not available, we'll coordinate a reschedule within 24 hours at no extra charge.
Do you offer bulk / corporate gifting?
Yes! We love corporate orders. We offer special pricing for bulk orders (10+ plants), custom branding on cards, and dedicated support. WhatsApp us at +91 9898081729 or email dreamgreennn@gmail.com.
Can I customize the pot or package?
All orders come in our signature Dream Green packaging. For special requests (custom pots, specific colors, corporate branding), please contact us and we'll do our best to accommodate.
How do I care for my new plant?
Every plant comes with a care card specific to that species. You can also check our Blog for detailed care guides. Still have questions? Message us on WhatsApp!
